Recipe - Duck with orange

Method of preparation

Peel the potatoes and cook until al dente. Remove all the pulp from the oranges and cut the peels into thin strips. Boil the peels in a pan for five minutes and strain them.Repeat the process 3 times to remove the bitterness from the peel. But keep the water from boiling and reserve the shells.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole duck clean
  • 500g of small potatoes
  • Juice of 3 oranges
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • 100g of sugar
  • 12 cups of oil
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • 20g of chopped parsley
